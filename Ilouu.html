Ilouu | Editorial Design

[2024]

This project involves creating an editorial object based on the theme of lists, where various sections, formats, and structures explore the concept of organization in all its forms. I chose to make this object a personal exploration, inspired by memory boxes and intimate archives, to create an enigmatic and introspective universe.

The editorial object is designed as a trompe-l'œil, made up of four distinct booklets, each representing an intimate fragment of myself—thoughts, tastes, name, and refuge. The pages and composition are carefully crafted to surprise, creating an immersive journey through a personal world where creative chaos meets meticulous organization.
