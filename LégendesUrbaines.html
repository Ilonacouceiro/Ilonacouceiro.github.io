Légendes Urbaines | Editorial design

[2023]

This project involves designing a program poster on the theme of Urban Legends. A foldable and reversible poster, it includes a table of contents, article excerpts, and images.

My approach draws inspiration from the concept of word-of-mouth, where image and text alternate through an unexpected layout. The use of varied typography and complementary colors creates a visual dialogue across the format. This design choice emphasizes the idea that each legend tells a unique story to be pieced together.
