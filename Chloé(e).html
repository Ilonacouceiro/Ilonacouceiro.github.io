Chloé(e) | Branding

[2023]

What if Chloé launched a new fragrance inspired by the South of France? Celebrating citrus fruits and provincial landscapes, this perfume is complemented by custom packaging and an advertising campaign.

Through a collaborative art direction, we aimed to create an innovative and fresh identity while maintaining Chloé's timeless elegance. The goal was to capture the essence of the Provencal landscape, offering a sensory and immersive experience for the audience.
