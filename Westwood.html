Westwood | Message Design

[2022]

This project consists of a double poster design for Vivienne Westwood, aimed at promoting a key product from the brand. The posters serve both to advertise the product and raise awareness against sexism.

My approach draws on Vivienne Westwood’s punk heritage, creating two posters that combine humor, provocation, and subtlety. The rebellious message against the hypersexualization of women is conveyed through a graphic oxymoron that blends vulgarity and elegance.
